About Redstone Register

What is Redstone?

Redstone is a highly complex and versatile system in the sandbox game Minecraft, serving as a rough equivalent to electrical engineering. Redstone empowers imagination and rewards logical thinking, allowing players to develop contraptions capable of automation, computation, and even trapping.

What is the Redstone Register?

Redstone Register is a PWA built on the Flask framework that aims to provide powerful and convenient access to information on all 46 redstone components in the massively popular sandbox game Minecraft.

Getting into a topic as vast and nuanced as redstone can be daunting. As such, Redstone Register aims to lower the barrier of entry into this vast field by presenting all the functional components of redstone in a comprehensive yet digestable format, opening a clear path into further discovery by piquing intrest.



Additionally, Redstone Register serves as a useful tool for more advanced players, effectively categorising all the aspects of physical redstone through search, filtering, and pinning, allowing for easy discovery, tracking, and further research. Every mega-project starts somewhere and in redstone, it starts with components.

How can I search for a component quickly?

Use the search field on the catalogue page. Results update as you type. You can also use filters to narrow by category, complexity, and obtainability. Sorting is available for alphabetical order, complexity, and obtainability.

Why do some recipes allow multiple types of wood?

Recipes that accept different wood types (like the jukebox) let you mix or match any wood. Many wooden recipes also accept any wood type as long as the recipe uses the same type throughout (such as doors).

Why are some components missing a recipe?

Some components are obtained from the environment (for example, redstone dust comes from ores) and do not require crafting.

Where do I learn more about a component?

Each component includes a wiki link inside its expanded details. The wiki has in-depth coverage of features, history, and usage.

Some recipes are missing! Where are they?

This catalogue covers all 46 redstone components in Minecraft. If you need items outside these, use the official Minecraft Wiki. Some components also have material variants (for example, different doors and pressure plates) that share the same overall entry.

How is complexity scored?

Complexity is scored from 1 to 4 based on how hard a component is to understand and use in a contraption. Higher scores indicate more difficult components.

How is obtainability scored?

Obtainability is scored from 1 to 4 based on how hard the component is to find or craft. Some require rare materials or dangerous environments.

Is this an official Minecraft site?

No, though if you thought it was that makes me very happy! This is simply a passion project to provide a genuinely useful service to the Minecraft community (much like the "official" wiki (which is technically also fan run)) whilst fufilling the requirments for a software engineering assessment task.
